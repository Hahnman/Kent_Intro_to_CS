In 1973, our founder Billy Bob Doe, broke down in his Oldsmobile on the border of a little town in Maine. After hours of trying to contact anyone he had a grand idea. In the small town he stopped in, he decided to set up shop. Later that day he used all his drug money and bought a loaf of bread, ham, lettuce, and other assorted condiments and opened up a small lemonade-stand-style sandwich place. His revolutionary sandwich recipe earned him major profits in the first day and a few months later, opened up a small shop that has now expanded into one of the largest chains around!"
